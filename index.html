How to play

Steer with the mouse/finger. Boost by holding click / Space / the ⚡ button — it costs length.

Eat glowing orbs to grow. Make rival snakes crash into your body to pop them — but never ram your own head into a body or a red hazard.

Power-ups: Shield = invincible, Magnet = pulls food, 2× = double score. Gold food = big points.

Modes: Survival (endless) · Score Attack (most points in 60s) · King of the Hill (hold the zone 10s) · Battle Royale (survive the shrinking ring — last snake wins).
