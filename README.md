
# Neon Slither

A fast-paced browser-based snake game featuring neon visuals, AI opponents, power-ups, hazards, multiple game modes, and mobile controls.

## Controls

| Control         | Action           |
| --------------- | ---------------- |
| Mouse / Touch   | Steer the snake  |
| Hold Left Click | Boost            |
| Space           | Boost            |
| Esc             | Pause or resume  |
| M               | Mute or unmute   |
| R               | Restart the game |

## How to Play

Eat glowing food orbs to increase your score and snake length.

Avoid crashing into enemy snakes, hazards, and the world boundaries. Make enemy snakes crash into your body to defeat them and collect the food they drop.

Boosting increases your speed but slowly reduces your snake length.
